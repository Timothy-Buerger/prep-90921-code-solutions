Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Lucas conceived Raiders of the Lost Ark in the early 1970's. Seeking to modernize the serial films of the 20th century, he developed the idea further with Kaufman, who suggested the Ark as the film's goal. Lucas eventually focused on developing his 1977 space opera Star Wars.

Temple of Doom

A prequel to the 1981 film Raiders of the Lost Ark. Indiana Jones is asked by desperate villagers to find a mystical stone and rescue their children from a Thuggee cult practicing child slavery, black magic, and ritualistic human sacrafice in honor of the goddess, Kali.

The Last Crusade

A 1989 American action-adventure film by Steven Spielberg. A sequel to Raiders of the Lost Ark. In 1938, Indiana searches for his father, a Holy Grail scholar, who has been captured by Nazis while on a journey to find the Holy Grail.
